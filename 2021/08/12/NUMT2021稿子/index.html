\1. Good morning, Ms Olive, would you please state your name for record?

Yes, my name is Jamie Olive. Olive, O-L-I-V-E.

OK, Ms. Olive. I’m going to ask you some questions. First, I will ask you something about yourself.

\2. Could you tell us something about your personal details?

I went to high school in United States and left for Europe after graduation. I studied at a culinary school in Paris before becoming a chef in a restaurant. I was the head chef at one of the best restaurants in Paris after a year.

Now let’s talk about how you were related with CG.

\3. How did you know CG?

I first met uncle Charlie when he came to my restaurant in 2005. He told me he was my uncle though my mother has probably never mentioned him .Then he offered me a current job as his company’s executive chef and the starting salary which was enough for me to quit my job right then and there.

\5. What did Grey tell you when he offered you a job?

He told me that I would travel the world with him to corporate event. However, I had to keep our family relationship a secret from everyone else since people may get jealous in his work.

\6. How was your relationship with G over the next few years?

Grey and I grew very close and I came to love him very much.

\7. What did you know about the private dinner in 2007?

The mansion was an open space, so I could hear the whole conversation. Grey said he gave 200grand to the professor Chartreuse and he found things about her academic corruption so Chartreuse promised not to say another word when the merger happened.

Grey handed me a large envelope contained the last will which means I would Inherit all the inheritance. I was shocked and told him that it was too much and I could never manage it all, but Grey said his associates could manage the business and I just need to enjoy it.

He had done some bad things in his life, and there were people who wanted to hurt him because of it. He wanted to make things right. And he asked me to help him prepare the meal.

I saw the butler enter the kitchen, followed by Chartreuse. They were having a conversation which lasted about 30 sec.

They stood quite far away from me and there were lots of noise, so I couldn’t hear what they were saying. But the conversation seemed to be pretty intense and there was a lot of pointing.Ch pointed to her watch and then went back to the dining room.

When chartreuse left, I asked the butler what chartreuse wanted, he snapped if I wanted to keep my job, I should keep my mouth shut.

after dinner, my uncle came to the kitchen and he said it was time for him to make the big announcement. It was the final words he spoke to me and the murder happened 20 mins later.

my uncle left with the door open, so I could see someone heading towards the utility closet and the private security guard of Crimson had shown up with down the hallway, standing outside the door to the conservatory. Besides, I saw someone going into the study and then there was a power cut.
